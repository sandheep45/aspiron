# Context-Aware AI Chat

Aspiron's AI chat provides contextual assistance across videos, notes, and quizzes.

## AI Chat Features

### Context Awareness

AI knows your:

- **Exam** - JEE, NEET, or PGT
- **Subject** - Your chosen subject
- **Topic** - Current learning topic
- **Timestamp** - Position in video
- **Notes** - Your personal notes

### Cross-Feature Integration

AI chat is available in:

- **Videos** - Explains current video content
- **Notes** - Enhances your notes
- **Quizzes** - Explains after submission
- **Tests** - Post-test analysis
- **Forum** - Community Bot integration

## Chat Capabilities

### Concept Explanation

- Simplify complex topics
- Explain in multiple ways
- Use examples
- Relate to other concepts

### Note Enhancement

- Summarize your notes
- Find related concepts
- Suggest additions
- Improve clarity

### Quiz Help (After Submission)

- Explain why answer is correct
- Break down solution
- Suggest related practice
- Identify gaps

### Test Analysis

- Review test performance
- Identify weak areas
- Suggest study plan
- Recommend resources

## AI Boundaries

### What AI Does

- Explains concepts you don't understand
- Simplifies difficult topics
- Gives examples
- Helps you learn

### What AI Does NOT Do

- Take tests for you
- Generate answers during exams
- Copy content from elsewhere
- Replace your thinking

## Using AI Chat

### Access Points

- Floating chat button
- Topic-specific AI
- Note enhancement
- Quiz explanation

### Conversation Flow

1. Ask question
2. AI considers context
3. Provides contextual answer
4. Suggest related topics
5. Offer further help

### Best Practices

- Be specific in questions
- Ask follow-up questions
- Request examples
- Use for understanding, not shortcuts

## AI and Learning

### Learning Focus

AI is designed for:

- Understanding concepts
- Filling knowledge gaps
- Providing examples
- Supporting recall

### Not a Shortcut

AI does NOT provide:

- Direct answers to questions
- Copy-paste solutions
- Exam answers
- Homework completion
