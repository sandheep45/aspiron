# Future Roadmap

Aspiron's MVP establishes the foundation. The roadmap describes where we're heading.

## Phase 1: MVP (Current)

**Focus**: Core learning infrastructure

- Video learning with timestamps and bookmarks
- Teacher and student notes (private + shareable)
- Practice quizzes with step-by-step solutions
- Chapter tests and full mock exams
- AI Recall Check for memory-aware revision
- Community Bot for peer support
- Peer discussion forum
- Progress tracking
- Exam-specific context (PGT, JEE, NEET)

## Phase 2: Enhanced Learning (Near-Term)

**Focus**: Deeper learning tools

### Advanced Notes

- Rich text editing for student notes
- Diagram and image support
- Notes linking and relationships
- Export to PDF

### Improved Quizzes

- Adaptive difficulty based on performance
- Detailed performance analytics per topic
- Question categorization by difficulty
- Timed section practice

### Better Videos

- Video annotations by teachers
- Chapter markers for long videos
- Picture-in-picture mode
- Download for offline viewing (limited)

### Teacher Tools

- Content upload dashboard
- Quiz creation and management
- Student progress view
- Announcement system

## Phase 3: AI Expansion (Mid-Term)

**Focus**: Intelligent learning assistance

### Personalized Study Paths

- AI analyzes strengths and weaknesses
- Generates customized study schedules
- Recommends content based on performance
- Adjusts difficulty dynamically

### Advanced AI Features

- AI-generated practice questions
- Conceptual relationship mapping
- Predictive performance modeling
- Auto-summarization of long content

### Smart Notifications

- Context-aware study reminders
- Learning optimal timing suggestions
- Proactive intervention when struggling
- Milestone celebrations

## Phase 4: Scale & Monetization

**Focus**: Sustainability and reach

### Monetization Model

- Subscription tiers (free + premium)
- Premium features:
  - Advanced analytics
  - Unlimited mock exams
  - Priority support
  - Offline access

### School/Institution Integration

- Batch student onboarding
- Teacher admin dashboards
- Class progress analytics
- Custom content for institutions

### Mobile Apps

- Native iOS app
- Native Android app
- Cross-platform sync
- Offline-first architecture

## Phase 5: Community & Ecosystem

**Focus**: Learning network effects

### Peer Learning

- Study groups
- Peer teaching opportunities
- Collaborative notes
- Mentorship connections

### Content Marketplace

- Premium content from experts
- Teacher monetization
- Quality-curated resources
- Community ratings

### Advanced Community

- Verified expert answers
- Gamified learning paths
- Achievement system (non-toxic)
- Peer challenges

## AI Evolution Philosophy

AI in Aspiron will evolve carefully:

### What AI Will NOT Do

- Replace student thinking
- Generate answers during exams
- Make high-stakes decisions
- Replace human teachers

### What AI Will Do

- Augment human learning
- Identify knowledge gaps
- Suggest optimal study strategies
- Provide contextual explanations
- Automate administrative tasks

### AI Trust Principles

- Transparency in AI decisions
- Student control over AI features
- No AI in high-stakes assessments
- Regular AI audits for bias
