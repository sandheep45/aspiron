# Community Bot

The Community Bot acts as a virtual tutor, providing academic guidance and emotional support.

## Bot Roles

### Virtual Tutor

- Answers questions
- Explains concepts
- Guides learning
- Suggests resources

### Moderator

- Summarizes long threads
- Detects duplicate doubts
- Nudges unanswered questions
- Keeps forum organized

### Emotional Support

- Normalizes struggle
- Provides encouragement
- Notices disengagement
- Offers support

### Academic Guide

- Asks guiding questions
- Provides hints, not solutions
- Points to resources
- Encourages critical thinking

## Bot Features

### Thread Summarization

Long discussion threads:

- Key points extracted
- Common answers identified
- Summary generated
- Easy to catch up

### Duplicate Detection

Similar doubts:

- Identified automatically
- Suggested existing answers
- Reduces repetition
- Connects related topics

### Nudging

Unanswered questions:

- Noticed by bot
- Nudges community to answer
- Suggests similar answered doubts
- Keeps engagement active

### Guiding Questions

Instead of giving answers:

- Asks "What have you tried?"
- Suggests "Look at this concept"
- Points to relevant video
- Encourages thinking

## Emotional Support Features

### Normalizing Struggle

Bot messages like:

- "You're not alone in this"
- "Many students find this challenging"
- "Struggling means you're learning"
- "Keep going, it gets easier"

### Disengagement Notice

Bot notices:

- Decreased activity
- Missed recall checks
- Low quiz scores
- Proactive outreach

### Encouragement

Positive messages:

- Celebrate small wins
- Acknowledge effort
- Remind of progress
- Build confidence

## Bot and Context

### Context-Aware

Bot knows your context:

- Your exam and subject
- Your current topics
- Your learning patterns
- Your progress

### Personalized Responses

Bot provides:

- Relevant answers
- Contextual suggestions
- Appropriate encouragement
- Targeted help

## Bot Boundaries

### What Bot Does

- Helps you learn
- Guides to resources
- Supports emotionally
- Moderates content

### What Bot Does NOT Do

- Give direct answers to homework
- Write your notes
- Take tests for you
- Replace human interaction
