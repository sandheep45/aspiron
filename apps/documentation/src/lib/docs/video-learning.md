# Video Learning

Aspiron's video learning system provides structured, interactive video content for effective learning.

## Video Features

### Playback Controls

- **Playback Speed** - 0.5x to 2x speed control
- **Resume** - Starts from where you left off
- **Skip Forward/Back** - 10-second navigation
- **Volume** - Standard volume control
- **Fullscreen** - Immersive viewing

### Video Navigation

- **Progress Bar** - Visual progress indicator
- **Chapter Markers** - Jump to specific sections
- **Timeline Thumbnails** - Preview frames on hover
- **Timestamps** - Click to jump to specific time

### Bookmarks

#### Create Bookmark

- Click bookmark icon while watching
- Add personal note to bookmark
- Timestamp automatically saved

#### Manage Bookmarks

- View all bookmarks in sidebar
- Click to jump to timestamp
- Edit bookmark notes
- Delete bookmarks

### Video Transcript

#### Transcript Features

- **Searchable Text** - Find content within video
- **Click to Jump** - Click text to jump to position
- **AI Integration** - Transcript used for AI context
- **Multi-language** - Transcript in selected language

#### Transcript Panel

- Toggle transcript panel
- Scroll through transcript
- Highlight current position
- Copy text to notes

## Video Organization

### Topic Videos

Each topic has associated videos:

- **Primary Video** - Main explanation
- **Supplementary Videos** - Additional examples
- **Practice Videos** - Problem-solving walkthroughs

### Video Playlists

Videos are organized as:

- Sequential playlists for chapters
- Related video suggestions
- Continue watching section
- Up next recommendations

## Video and Notes

### Timestamped Notes

Create notes while watching:

1. Click "Add Note" button
2. Note is auto-timestamped
3. Click timestamp to return to exact moment
4. Notes linked to video and topic

### Notes Integration

- Notes appear in sidebar during playback
- Click note to jump to video position
- Notes sync with video progress
- Reference specific video sections

## Video and AI

### AI Features

AI enhances video learning:

- **Transcript Analysis** - Indexes content for search
- **Concept Explanations** - Explains complex parts
- **Summary Generation** - Creates video summaries
- **Related Suggestions** - Recommends related videos

### AI Boundaries

AI never:
- Watches videos for you
- Takes notes for you
- Answers based on partial understanding

## Video Quality

### Quality Options

- **Auto** - Based on connection
- **1080p** - High definition
- **720p** - Standard definition
- **480p** - Low bandwidth

### Offline Viewing

> **Planned**: Offline download feature coming in later phases

## Video Accessibility

### Accessibility Features

- **Keyboard Navigation** - Control playback with keyboard
- **Screen Reader Support** - Transcript accessible
- **Contrast Options** - High contrast mode (future)
- **Captions** - Subtitles in supported languages
